Introduction

Minecraft is a PC game which was created by Markus "Notch" Persson. It is a sandbox game with worlds created from blocks. Most people play the game for its large focus on building, but others like to play the game for its other aspects, such as player vs player combat(PVP), trying their luck with mining, socialising on, online servers, or even coding modifications for the game.

This website will be focusing on the PVP aspect of the game. It will feature general information about PVP, different techniques and tips, types of servers and a damage resistance calculator.

Note: Pressing - "up, up, down, down, left, right, left, right, b, a" With your keyboard, on any page will activate an easter egg.
