General Information

Swords

In Minecraft there are five types of swords, which are all made of different materials. These materials are wood, gold, stone, iron, diamond. Wooden swords deal 5 damage (2.5 hearts), Golden swords deal 5 damage as well, Stone deals 6 damage (3 hearts), Iron deals 7 damage (3.5 hearts) and Diamond deals 8 damage (4 hearts).

Armour

In Minecraft there are five different types of armour. There is Leather, which is the worst type, then gold, chainmail, iron and diamond, which is the best. The damage resistance of the armour types can be calculated by using the damage resistance calculator on this website.

Enchantments

There are lots of enchantments in Minecraft. The main ones that apply to PVP are - sharpness, fire aspect, knockback, protection and thorns. Sharpness, fire aspect and knockback can be applied to swords, and protection and thorns can be applied to armour.

Sharpness

Sharpness makes your sword deal more damage. Each level adds 1.25 damage per level.

Fire aspect

Fire aspect sets your opponent on fire, with each level setting them on fire for longer.

Knockback

Knockback knocks back your opponent more than a sword more than a sword without it. With knockback 2, you can knockback your enemies extremely far, especially if they are jumping.

Thorns

Armour with the thorns enchantment will have a chance to damage your enemy when they hit you.

Potions

Minecraft has potions, which in PVP, will give you a great advantage. With the right potions, you can beat an enemy who has much better items than you.

Health

Health potions heal you when they are thrown on the ground. Each level heals you by 4 health (2 hearts).

Speed

Speed potions increase the speed at which you move. Each level increases your walking speed by 20%.

Strength

Increases damage dealt by 130%. With strength 2, you can deal 260% more damage, which makes this potion one of the most useful.

Regeneration

Regenerates 1 health (0.5 hearts) every 2.5 seconds.

Fire Resistance

Grants immunity to fire.

Enderpearls

Enderpearls are an object which can be thrown and will teleport you to the location at which they land. They can be extremely useful when running away from someone or getting places fast.
